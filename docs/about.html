About the HUB

The HUB is a needs-responsive support structure for grassroots organizers across so-called Canada. You tell us your needs, questions, and pain points that are blocking the group from moving forward and we respond with relevant offerings. To learn about these in more detail, take a look at the Offerings page and our Upcoming events and releases.

Funding and governance

Funding for the HUB is entirely provided by Canadian philanthropic foundations. The HUB is formally a project of the Small Change Fund, an Ontario-based charity organization.

The HUB is run by its Directors and their larger team, in constant consultation with the climate justice organizers we seek to serve. The HUB also maintains relationships with Vision Team Advisors, that bring perspectives from different parts of the movement ecosystem and help the HUB show up in the best way possible.
